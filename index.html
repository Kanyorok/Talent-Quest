    The Worthy Pursuit

    The Worthy Pursuit Initiative CBO is a dynamic organization under the umbrella of Talent Quest Sports, committed to fostering love, unity, prosperity, and continuity within the community. Our initiative aims to empower children to reach their full potential through sports and recreational activities. By creating a supportive environment, we encourage the development of essential life skills, teamwork, and personal growth. At The Worthy Pursuit Initiative, we believe in the transformative power of sports to inspire a brighter future, promoting well-being and unity among youth while ensuring the continued growth and prosperity of our community.

    Worthy Pursuit Mission

    To be a development community based organization that fosters unity, love, prosperity and continuity

    Worthy Pursuit Vision

    To enable children from less privileged families in Kenya to achieve their full potential by providing access to education including essential resources so that they can grow up to be independent but socially conscious individuals and participate in sustainable development of their society through sports and recreational activities.
